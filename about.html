    • About Us

    Welcome to Michigan’s Premier Amusement Park, where adventure meets fun for families and thrill-seekers alike!


    Who We Are

    Founded in 1995, our amusement park has become a beloved destination for Michigan residents and visitors from around the world. With a combination of thrilling rides, family-friendly activities, and seasonal events, we promise an unforgettable experience.


    Fun Facts About Our Park

    • We are home to the tallest roller coaster in Michigan, standing at 250 feet!
    • Our Ferris Wheel offers panoramic views of Lake Michigan on clear days.
    • Every year, over 1 million visitors walk through our gates.
    • Our park features over 50 rides and attractions, including kid-friendly adventures and extreme thrill rides.
    • The park mascot, "Sammy the Squirrel", has been entertaining guests for over 20 years.

    Our Mission

    We aim to provide safe, exciting, and memorable experiences for visitors of all ages. Whether you're here for thrills, relaxation, or family fun, we have something for everyone!
